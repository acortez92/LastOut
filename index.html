Last Out: Elegy of a Green Beret.

Tampa Theatre

On the evening of May 17, 2022 at the Tampa Theater, Avantus Federal and Far Ridgeline Engagements are co-hosting an exclusive charity event and screening of Last Out: Elegy of a Green Beret, a film by The Heroes Journey. The film, featuring all-veteran cast and crew, is a story dedicated to those who fell, the warriors who held them in their final moments and the families who carry their memories forward.

This event will feature members of the Last Out cast, several Tampa-based charities that directly support our nation’s most elite warriors as they deal with the physical and mental challenges of their service, those that made the ultimate sacrifice and the Gold Star families that continue to deal with unimaginable loss.

Proceeds of this event will be split amongst the following charitable organizations: Special Operations Warrior Foundation, Operation Recovery, Project Transition USA and The Heroes Journey.

Please join us for a memorable night, help us raise awareness of the cost of war carried by our brave men and women in uniform and their families, and help us to keep our solemn promise to never forget their sacrifices.
